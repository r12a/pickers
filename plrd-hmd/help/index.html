List of character apps

Updated 28-Feb-2021

This Unicode character app allows you to produce or analyse runs of Miao text. character apps are especially useful for people who don't know a script well, as characters are displayed in ways that aid identification.

If something is broken or missing raise an issue. For version information see the Github commit list. The pictures of the UI used in this page are taken from a variety of character apps.

Inputting characters

Includes most of the characters in the Unicode Miao block.

To analyse text, copy & paste it into the large text area and select the desired control(s).

To produce Miao script text in the text area, click on one of the orange items in the selection area that resembles the shape of the character you are looking for. Then click on one of the grey shapes that appears below to add the character to the text area. The orange ellipsis (...) gives access to a mix of characters with shapes that didn't fit elsewhere.
